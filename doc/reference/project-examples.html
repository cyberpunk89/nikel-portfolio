What Makes a Good Project?

✅ Do Include

  • Clear problem statement (The Challenge)
  • Research and insights
  • Design process explanation
  • Screenshots/mockups
  • Outcome and results
  • External link to full case study (Behance/Medium)

❌ Avoid

  • Very brief content (< 30 lines)
  • No clear structure
  • Missing images
  • No problem/solution clarity

Quick Reference

  • Show a project: Remove hidden field or set to false
  • Hide a project: Add hidden: true
  • Link to experience: Match client to experience in About section
  • Thumbnail: Place at public/media/projects/[slug]/thumbnail.png
